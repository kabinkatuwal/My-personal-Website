Chicken Momo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground chicken
  • 2 cups finely chopped cabbage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Momo wrappers

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients except wrappers
  2. Place spoonful in center of wrapper
  3. Fold and pleat edges to seal
  4. Steam for 10-12 minutes
  5. Serve with tomato achar
